# YouTube Channel Shorts

YouTube Shorts has become one of the fastest-growing content formats on the platform, with billions of daily views competing directly with TikTok and Instagram Reels. For creators and marketers, understanding what works in short-form video requires data - which topics get the most views, how often top channels post Shorts, and what titles and formats perform best. But YouTube does not export Shorts data in bulk, and the API makes it difficult to isolate Shorts from regular videos.

## What it extracts

- **Position** — The ranking position of the YouTube Short in the channel's Shorts tab.
- **Title** — The title of the YouTube Short as displayed on the channel page.
- **Views** — The number of views the Short has received.
- **Image** — The thumbnail image URL for the Short.
- **Link** — The direct link to the Short on YouTube.

## What you can do with it

- Every Short from a channel's Shorts tab - titles, view counts, and dates - captured in structured rows for sorting and analysis.
- Competitor Shorts strategy analysis: see posting frequency, view patterns, and which topics drive the most engagement in short-form content.
- Trend identification by comparing Shorts performance across channels in your niche to spot formats and topics gaining traction.
- Content planning intelligence that tells you how many Shorts to post, what titles work, and when to publish based on real performance data.
