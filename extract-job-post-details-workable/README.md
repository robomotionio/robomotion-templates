# Workable Job Details

Workable is one of the most widely adopted applicant tracking systems, powering the career pages of over 27,000 companies across industries. When you visit a company's careers page and the URL contains 'apply.workable.com' or the page has Workable's characteristic layout, you are looking at Workable-hosted job postings. These postings contain structured data that is valuable for job seekers comparing opportunities, recruiters benchmarking competitor roles, and analysts studying hiring patterns.

## What it extracts

- **Job Title** — The position title.
- **Company Name** — The name of the hiring organization.
- **Department Name** — The team or function the role belongs to.
- **Location** — Work location (office, remote, or hybrid).
- **Workplace** — Work arrangement details.
- **Employment Type** — Full-time, part-time, contract, or internship.
- **Description** — The full job description text.
- **Requirements** — Listed qualifications and skills.
- **Benefits** — Compensation, perks, and benefits offered.
- **Date** — Job posting date.
- **Image URL** — Company logo or job posting image.
- **About C** — Company information and overview.
- **Company's Website** — Direct link to the company website.

## What you can do with it

- Full job postings from Workable-powered career pages - titles, descriptions, requirements, and metadata captured as structured data for comparison.
- Cross-company comparison: extract the same role from different Workable-using companies to compare requirements, responsibilities, and how each employer positions the opportunity.
- ATS ecosystem intelligence: Workable's 27,000+ customers represent a significant slice of the hiring market. Data from Workable postings reveals hiring patterns across this ecosystem.
- Requirement analysis: extract qualification sections from multiple postings to identify which skills, certifications, and experience levels employers consistently require for specific roles.
