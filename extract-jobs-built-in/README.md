# Built In Jobs

Built In has become one of the most valuable platforms for tech professionals because it pairs job listings with structured company data across major US tech hubs - Chicago, Austin, New York, San Francisco, Seattle, Boston, Colorado, and Los Angeles. Built In gives you the information to evaluate an employer and understand the role requirements. When you see a job on Built In, you also see the company details, required experience level, employment type, and when the role was posted.

## What it extracts

- **Position** — The ranking position of the job listing.
- **Job Title** — The position title.
- **Job Link** — Direct URL to the job listing on Built In.
- **Company** — The tech company name.
- **When Posted** — The date the job listing was published.
- **Location** — Office location or remote designation.
- **Job Type** — Employment type (full-time, contract, etc.).
- **Employees** — Company headcount or size range.
- **Salary** — Compensation range when disclosed.
- **Years Experience** — Required or preferred experience level.
- **Company Image** — Logo or company thumbnail image.
