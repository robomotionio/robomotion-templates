# ProBlogger Job Details

ProBlogger's job board has been the go-to destination for freelance writers, content creators, and blogging professionals for over a decade. What sets it apart is curation - employers pay to post, which filters out low-quality listings and scams that plague free job boards. The roles span blog writing, copywriting, content strategy, editing, ghostwriting, and technical writing.

## What it extracts

- **Job Title** — The position or gig title.
- **Company** — The company or individual posting the job.
- **Company Link** — Direct URL to the company's website or job posting page.
- **Date Posted** — When the job listing was published on ProBlogger.
- **Location** — Geographic location or remote status of the role.
- **Job Type** — Employment type (freelance, part-time, full-time, project-based, etc.).
- **Category** — The content or writing category (blog posts, copywriting, technical writing, etc.).
- **Company Type** — The type of organization (agency, startup, enterprise, individual, etc.).
- **Description** — Full job description and key details.
