# We Work Remotely Jobs

We Work Remotely processes millions of monthly visitors looking for remote work, making it the highest-traffic remote job board in the world. Its category pages - programming, design, marketing, sales, DevOps, customer support, and executive - each contain dozens of active listings from companies with established distributed teams. Scanning these categories manually means spending hours clicking through individual posts.

## What it extracts

- **Position** — The sequential listing number.
- **Company Name** — The hiring organization.
- **Job Title** — The position title.
- **Job Type** — Employment classification (full-time, part-time, contract, etc.).
- **Location** — Geographic preference or restriction.
- **Date Posted** — When the listing was published.
- **Job Link** — Direct URL to the job posting.

## What you can do with it

- Full category or board extraction in one pass - capture every active listing on We Work Remotely instead of checking categories individually.
- Structured for sorting and filtering: extracted data can be organized by category, company, region, and posting date in your spreadsheet or database.
- Historical tracking capability: run the same extraction weekly or monthly to build a time series of remote job market data from the largest single source.
- Company pattern recognition: see which companies post most frequently on We Work Remotely. Consistent posters are the most committed to remote hiring.

## How it works

Just run the flow. It loads the source, extracts the fields listed above, and saves them as a CSV in your home folder.

## Frequently asked questions

**How is this different from the We Work Remotely detail scraper?**

This robot extracts multiple listings from search results and category pages at once. The detail scraper extracts the full description from one individual posting.

**Can I extract specific categories only?**

Yes. Navigate to a specific We Work Remotely category page before copying the URL. The robot extracts only listings from that category.

**How often should I extract?**

Weekly extraction captures most new listings. For fast-moving categories like programming, daily extraction catches opportunities before competition builds.

**Does it include salary data?**

When We Work Remotely listings include salary information in the summary, the robot extracts it. Not all listings disclose compensation at the board level.
