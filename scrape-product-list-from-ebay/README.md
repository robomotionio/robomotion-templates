# eBay Product Listings

eBay's 1.7 billion listings make it the most price-diverse product marketplace on the internet. Unlike fixed-price retailers, eBay combines auction-style bidding with Buy It Now listings, creating real-time market pricing signals that no other platform replicates. Each eBay search result displays the item title, current price or bid amount, product image, and direct listing link.

## What it extracts

- **Position** — Ranking position of the listing in search results.
- **Title** — Full listing title as posted by the seller.
- **Image** — Product thumbnail image from the listing.
- **Sale Price** — Buy It Now price or current bid amount.
- **Link** — Direct URL to the eBay listing page.

## What you can do with it

- Real-time secondary market pricing: eBay's auction results are the most accurate measure of what buyers will actually pay for products. Extract listings and sold prices to calibrate your inventory valuation and pricing strategy against real market demand.
- Reseller arbitrage identification: Price gaps between eBay and retail create arbitrage opportunities. Extract eBay listings for product categories you source to find undervalued inventory or spot where retail prices exceed eBay market rates.
- Competitor seller analysis: Extract search results filtered to specific eBay sellers to analyze competitor inventory depth, pricing patterns, and product focus areas.
- Condition-based price intelligence: eBay uniquely surfaces new, used, and refurbished prices side-by-side. Extract across condition tiers to understand the price spread and identify which conditions drive the most buyer interest.

## How it works

Enter the requested input when prompted. The flow opens the target page, extracts the fields listed above, and saves them as a CSV in your home folder.

## Frequently asked questions

**Can I extract completed and sold eBay listings?**

Yes. Filter eBay search results for 'Sold Items' or 'Completed Listings' before copying the URL. Sold listing data reveals actual transaction prices - the most accurate market valuation signal.

**Does this work for eBay Motors or other specialty categories?**

Yes. The robot works with any eBay search or category URL, including eBay Motors, eBay Business & Industrial, and other specialty storefronts.

**Can I track a specific seller's listings?**

Yes. Navigate to a seller's eBay store page, apply any filters needed, and paste that URL. The robot extracts all listings displayed.
