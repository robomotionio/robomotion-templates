# DuckDuckGo Search Results

DuckDuckGo has grown from a niche privacy alternative to a mainstream search engine with over 100 million daily searches. Unlike Google, DuckDuckGo doesn't track users or personalize results based on search history, which means its results represent a more neutral view of search rankings - what you see is what everyone sees. This makes DuckDuckGo results particularly interesting for SEO analysis because they remove the personalization variable that makes Google results inconsistent across users.

## What it extracts

- **Position** — Ranking position in search results.
- **Title** — Page title as displayed.
- **Description** — Snippet or meta description text.
- **Link** — Destination page URL.
- **Ad Title** — Title text of paid search advertisements.
- **Ad Description** — Description or snippet text of paid search advertisements.
- **Ad Link** — Destination URL of paid search advertisements.

## What you can do with it

- Non-personalized ranking baseline: DuckDuckGo shows the same results to every user. Extract to get a clean baseline of rankings without the noise of Google's personalization.
- Privacy-focused audience visibility: DuckDuckGo users are growing and tend to be technically sophisticated. Extract rankings to understand your visibility with this valuable audience segment.
- Multi-engine SEO completeness: Comprehensive SEO tracking requires monitoring more than just Google. Extract DuckDuckGo results alongside Google and Bing for complete search visibility coverage.
- Algorithm diversity analysis: DuckDuckGo uses different ranking signals than Google. Extract results for the same keywords to understand how different engines evaluate your content.

## How it works

Enter the requested input when prompted. The flow opens the target page, extracts the fields listed above, and saves them as a CSV in your home folder.

## Frequently asked questions

**How does DuckDuckGo ranking differ from Google?**

DuckDuckGo uses Bing's index as a primary source, plus its own crawler and curated sources. It doesn't personalize results, so rankings are consistent across all users and sessions.

**Is DuckDuckGo traffic significant enough to track?**

With over 100 million daily searches and a growing, technically sophisticated user base, DuckDuckGo represents a meaningful and growing traffic source, especially for tech and privacy-related content.

**Can I extract DuckDuckGo results from specific regions?**

DuckDuckGo offers some regional search options. Adjust the URL parameters for region-specific results and the robot will extract whatever the page displays, including organic results and advertisements.
