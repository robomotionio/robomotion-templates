# SEEK Businesses for Sale

SEEK Business is Australia's premier marketplace for buying and selling businesses, with thousands of active listings spanning every industry from hospitality to professional services. For buyers, brokers, and analysts who need to track deal flow or analyze market pricing, browsing listings one at a time on the SEEK Business website is not practical when you need data at scale.

## What it extracts

- **Position** — The ranking position of the listing in the search results.
- **Listing Title** — The name and headline of the business listing.
- **Location** — The city and state where the business operates.
- **Price** — The asking price in Australian dollars.
- **Business Type** — The industry or category description of the business for sale.
- **Description** — The listing summary describing the business and the opportunity.
- **Listing URL** — The direct link to the full listing on SEEK Business.
- **Location URL** — The link to the location or business profile page.
- **Image** — The listing thumbnail or featured image.

## What you can do with it

- Full search result pages of businesses for sale captured in structured columns - price, turnover, industry, and location - ready for analysis.
- Side-by-side comparison of listings across industries and regions without toggling between dozens of SEEK Business pages.
- Automated deal monitoring through scheduled runs that flag new listings matching your investment criteria each week.
- Direct data flow into spreadsheets, Airtable databases, or CRM tools through built-in integrations.

Enter the requested input when prompted. The flow opens the target page, extracts the fields listed above, and saves them as a CSV in your home folder.
