# SEEK Business Courses

SEEK is the dominant jobs and training platform in Australia and New Zealand, and its job listings section aggregates thousands of professional opportunities from employers across all industries. For recruiters, job boards, and career researchers, this data is valuable for comparing positions - but SEEK does not offer bulk export and browsing through listings one by one is slow.

## What it extracts

- **Position** — The ranking or listing number of the job posting on the results page.
- **Role Name** — The job title or position name.
- **Typical Salary** — The advertised salary range or typical compensation for the role.
- **Image** — The employer logo or job listing thumbnail image.

## What you can do with it

- Full course catalogs from SEEK - titles, providers, durations, and prices captured in one extraction instead of clicking through hundreds of individual listings.
- Training comparison across providers: sort courses by price, duration, or delivery mode to find the best fit for your learning goals or team development budget.
- Market research for the professional education sector in ANZ - understand pricing trends, popular topics, and provider concentration.
- Procurement intelligence for L&D teams comparing training options across multiple providers and qualification levels.

## How it works

Enter the requested input when prompted. The flow opens the target page, extracts the fields listed above, and saves them as a CSV in your home folder.
