We Work Remotely is the largest remote work community in the world, with millions of monthly visitors and thousands of job listings from companies that have made distributed work a core part of their operating model. The platform covers everything from programming and design to sales, marketing, customer support, and executive roles. Companies that post on We Work Remotely include some of the best-known remote-first organizations in tech.

## What it extracts

- **Date Posted** — When the job listing was published.
- **Job Title** — The position title.
- **Job Type** — Employment type (full-time, part-time, contract, etc.).
- **Job Category** — The job category (programming, design, marketing, DevOps, etc.).
- **Location** — Geographic eligibility or preference.
- **Company** — The hiring organization.
- **Company Location** — The company's headquarters or primary location.
- **Company Website** — The company's official website URL.
- **Job Description** — Full details about the role and requirements.
- **Link to Apply** — Direct URL to submit an application.
- **Position** — Position rank or identifier in the listing.
- **Related Company** — Other companies with similar openings.
- **Related Job Title** — Similar job titles on the platform.
- **Related Job Type** — Employment types for related positions.
- **Related Job Link** — URLs to related job listings.
