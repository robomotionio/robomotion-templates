SEEK is the dominant online employment marketplace in Australia and New Zealand, with over 170,000 active job listings at any given time. SEEK listings include salary data more frequently than most global job boards - Australian employment culture increasingly expects salary transparency in postings.

## What it extracts

- **Position** — The position number in the search results.
- **Job Title** — The position title as listed on SEEK.
- **Company Name** — The hiring employer.
- **Company Jobs Link** — Direct link to the employer's job listings page on SEEK.
- **Location** — The job location (city, state, or region in Australia/NZ).
- **Requirements** — Key qualifications and requirements for the role.
- **Description** — A detailed description of the role from the listing.
- **Salary** — The advertised salary range (when disclosed by the employer).
- **Logo** — The company logo image.
- **Job link** — Direct URL to the full job listing on SEEK.
- **Listing Date** — The date the job was posted on SEEK.
