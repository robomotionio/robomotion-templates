# Trendyol Product Details

Trendyol is Turkey's dominant e-commerce platform, processing over 1.5 million orders daily and listing products from tens of thousands of sellers across fashion, electronics, beauty, home goods, and groceries. Individual product pages on Trendyol contain granular detail that listing pages skip - exact pricing, full product descriptions, comprehensive customer ratings and review links, material composition, and additional product specifications.

## What it extracts

- **Product Name** — Full product title.
- **Price** — Active selling price in TL.
- **Total Reviews** — Total number of customer reviews.
- **Total Rating** — Average customer review score.
- **Rate Score** — Detailed rating breakdown by score.
- **Reviews Links** — Direct links to customer reviews.
- **Product Information** — Full text description and product details.
- **Additional info** — Extra product specifications and attributes.
- **Material** — Material composition and fabric information.

## What you can do with it

- Granular pricing intelligence: See the exact current price, original price, and discount percentage. Track how sellers adjust prices relative to their listed retail values.
- Seller identification: Every Trendyol product page names the seller with their marketplace rating. Build structured seller databases for partnership or competitive analysis.
- Size and variant availability: Fashion and apparel products show which sizes and colors are in stock. Monitor competitor stock levels without placing test orders.
- Review depth analysis: Extract review scores and counts to understand product market reception. High review volume with strong ratings signals proven product-market fit.

## How it works

Enter the requested input when prompted. The flow opens the target page, extracts the fields listed above, and saves them as a CSV in your home folder.
