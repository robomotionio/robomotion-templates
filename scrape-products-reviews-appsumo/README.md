# AppSumo Product Reviews

AppSumo buyers are among the most opinionated software users on the internet. When they purchase a lifetime deal, they have a direct financial stake in the product's quality and longevity - which means their reviews are unusually detailed, honest, and actionable. Each review on an AppSumo deal page includes the reviewer's name, their star rating (1-5), the review title, the full text of their review, the date it was posted, their avatar image, and a direct link to the review.

## What it extracts

- **Position** — The review's position or ranking on the AppSumo deal page.
- **User Name** — Display name of the buyer who submitted the review.
- **Date** — When the review was posted on AppSumo.
- **Title** — The headline or summary title of the review.
- **Review** — Full text of the buyer's detailed review.
- **Avatar** — Profile avatar image of the reviewer.
- **Review Link** — Direct URL link to the individual review.
- **Rating** — Star rating from 1 to 5 stars.

## What you can do with it

- Pre-purchase due diligence: Extract all reviews for a deal before buying. Read unfiltered feedback from verified purchasers about real-world performance, support quality, and feature completeness.
- Sentiment pattern analysis: Aggregate review ratings and text to identify recurring themes - what users consistently praise, what they criticize, and what features generate the most polarized reactions.
- Competitive product research: Extract reviews for competitor tools on AppSumo. Understand their weaknesses and customer pain points to differentiate your own product or purchasing decisions.
- Founder product feedback loop: If your product is listed on AppSumo, extract reviews regularly to prioritize bug fixes, feature requests, and support improvements based on actual buyer feedback.

## How it works

Enter the requested input when prompted. The flow opens the target page, extracts the fields listed above, and saves them as a CSV in your home folder.

## Frequently asked questions

**How is this different from the AppSumo product listings scraper?**

The product listings scraper (page 228) extracts deal-level data - name, price, discount, aggregate rating - across multiple products. This robot goes deeper: it extracts every individual review for a single deal page, including review titles, full review text, reviewer avatars, and direct review links.

**Can I extract reviews sorted by most recent or most helpful?**

AppSumo allows sorting reviews by different criteria. Sort the reviews as desired before copying the URL, and the robot will extract whatever order the page displays.

**What can I do with the review links extracted?**

The review links allow you to reference specific reviews directly, share them with your team, or track individual reviewer feedback over time for ongoing product evaluation.
