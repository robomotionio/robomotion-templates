# Airbnb Property Details

Airbnb hosts over 7 million active listings worldwide, making it the largest dataset of short-term rental properties available anywhere. Each property listing page contains detailed information that investors, property managers, and market researchers need: nightly rates with seasonal pricing, comprehensive guest review scores including cleanliness, accuracy, check-in, communication, location, and value ratings, individual guest reviews with reviewer names and profiles, complete amenity lists, location details, and property photos.

## What it extracts

- **Overall Rating** — Average guest review score (1-5 scale).
- **Cleanliness** — Guest rating for property cleanliness.
- **Accuracy** — Guest rating for listing accuracy.
- **Check-in** — Guest rating for check-in experience.
- **Communication** — Guest rating for host communication.
- **Location** — Guest rating for location quality.
- **Value** — Guest rating for value for money.
- **Price** — Current nightly rate.
- **Position** — Ranking position in results or on page.
- **Reviewer Name** — Name of the guest who left the review.
- **Reviewer Details** — Guest profile information and metadata.
- **Rating** — Individual review rating score.
- **Review Text** — Full text of the guest review.
- **Profile Link** — URL link to reviewer's profile.
- **Amenity** — Individual amenity offered (WiFi, kitchen, parking, etc.).
- **imgURL** — Direct URL to property or amenity images.

## What you can do with it

- Competitive pricing intelligence: Extract nightly rates from comparable properties in your market. Know exactly what similar listings charge before setting or adjusting your own prices.
- Review analysis at scale: Extract review scores and guest feedback to understand what guests value and complain about. Learn from competitor reviews to improve your own listing.
- Amenity benchmarking: See which amenities top-performing listings offer. Identify amenity gaps in your listing relative to competitors that could boost bookings.
- Host performance tracking: Extract Superhost status, response rates, and hosting tenure. Understand the profile of successful hosts in your market.

## How it works

Enter the requested input when prompted. The flow opens the target page, extracts the fields listed above, and saves them as a CSV in your home folder.

## Frequently asked questions

**Can I track Airbnb pricing over time?**

Schedule the robot to extract the same listing periodically. Compare extractions to see how hosts adjust pricing for seasons, events, and demand.

**Does it extract actual booking data?**

The robot extracts publicly visible listing information including nightly rates, guest ratings (overall, cleanliness, accuracy, communication, location, value), individual reviews with reviewer details, and amenities. Actual booking calendars and occupancy data require different approaches.

**Can I extract listings from Airbnb search results?**

This robot targets individual listing pages for deep data. For search result pages with multiple listings, use the URL of the listing you want.

**Does it work internationally?**

Yes. Airbnb listings from any country or city work with this robot. Pricing will reflect the listing's local currency.
