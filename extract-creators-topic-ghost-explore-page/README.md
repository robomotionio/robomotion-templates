# Ghost Explore Creators by Topic

Ghost Explore organizes independent publications into topic categories - technology, business, culture, design, food, and dozens more. Each topic page lists the publications tagged to that subject, creating a curated directory of indie newsletters and blogs. For content professionals researching the newsletter landscape, topic pages provide a more structured starting point than keyword search.

## What it extracts

- **Position** — The ranking position of the creator on the Ghost Explore topic page.
- **Creator Name** — The name of the indie creator or publication.
- **Creator description** — How the creator or publication describes itself on Ghost Explore.
- **Website** — Link to the creator's website or publication page on Ghost.
- **MRR** — Monthly Recurring Revenue or audience size indicator for the publication.
- **Number of Members** — The subscriber count or community size of the publication.
- **imgURL** — Profile image or logo of the creator or publication.

## What you can do with it

- Complete topic directories from Ghost - every publication in a category extracted with author names, descriptions, and profile links.
- Niche landscape mapping: see how many independent publications cover a specific topic on Ghost, who the most prominent creators are, and what angles they take.
- Curated reading list creation: extract topic page listings and filter to build personalized newsletter collections for your team or audience.
- Publishing opportunity identification by finding topic categories with high reader interest but few publications - underserved niches for new newsletters.
