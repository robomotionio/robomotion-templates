# IMDb Upcoming Releases

IMDb's upcoming movies section is the entertainment industry's most comprehensive calendar of future theatrical and streaming releases. Updated continuously as distributors confirm dates, these listings include film titles, genres, principal cast members, and official poster artwork. IMDb's organized release calendar provides ranking positions that help identify the most anticipated upcoming releases.

## What it extracts

- **Position** — Ranking position in the upcoming releases list.
- **Title** — Official film title.
- **Genre 1** — Primary genre classification.
- **Genre 2** — Secondary genre classification.
- **Genre 3** — Tertiary genre classification.
- **Lead Actor** — Principal cast member.
- **Supporting Actor 1** — Secondary cast member.
- **Supporting Actor 2** — Tertiary cast member.
- **Supporting Actor 3** — Quaternary cast member.
- **Poster Image** — Official movie poster thumbnail.
- **Movie Link** — Direct link to IMDb movie page.

## What you can do with it

- Complete release calendar: Extract every upcoming title with confirmed release dates. Build comprehensive calendars that cover months of future releases in one extraction.
- Cast and crew intelligence: Director and star names come with each listing. Extract to analyze which talent combinations generate the most anticipation or track specific actors' upcoming projects.
- Genre-based planning: Each title includes genre tags. Extract and filter to focus on specific categories - action, comedy, horror, drama - for targeted content or marketing strategies.
- Audience anticipation signals: IMDb popularity and user interest metrics indicate which upcoming films are generating buzz. Extract to prioritize coverage of high-interest releases.

## How it works

Just run the flow. It loads the source, extracts the fields listed above, and saves them as a CSV in your home folder.

## Frequently asked questions

**How far ahead does IMDb list upcoming movies?**

IMDb lists upcoming releases several months to over a year in advance, depending on how early studios announce and confirm distribution dates.

**Are streaming-only releases included?**

Yes. IMDb's upcoming releases include both theatrical and streaming platform exclusives, as long as they have confirmed distribution.

**What information is extracted for each movie?**

The robot extracts the position ranking, title, three genre classifications, four cast members, poster image, and direct link to the IMDb movie page for each upcoming release.
