# Framer Template Details

Framer's template marketplace is growing fast, and design teams evaluating templates face a time-consuming comparison process. Each template page has its own layout with varying levels of detail about pricing, features, responsive breakpoints, CMS integration, publish dates, page counts, and what the creator includes in the package. Clicking through dozens of template pages to compare options is slow, especially when you need to evaluate templates across multiple categories.

## What it extracts

- **Template Name** — The title of the Framer template as listed on the marketplace.
- **Creator** — The name or studio of the template's creator.
- **Creator Link** — The URL to the creator's profile or portfolio.
- **Summary** — A brief overview of the template's purpose and key benefits.
- **Price** — The listed price for the template, including any sale pricing if active.
- **Preview Link** — Link to the live preview or demo site for the template.
- **Description** — The creator's description of what the template offers and who it is for.
- **Publish Date** — The date the template was published or last updated on the marketplace.
- **Template Pages** — The number of pages or sections included in the template.
- **Position** — The ranking or position of the template within its category or search results.
- **Category Name** — The marketplace category or tag assigned to the template.
- **Category Link** — The URL link to the template's category page.
- **Feature** — The specific features the template includes (CMS, dark mode, animations, etc.).

## What you can do with it

- Structured comparison of Framer templates - names, creators, prices, features, and categories organized into rows instead of scattered across individual marketplace pages.
- Pricing intelligence for template creators who want to see how their rates compare to similar templates in the same Framer category.
- Faster template selection for design teams by building a filterable spreadsheet of options rather than clicking through template pages one at a time.
- Market analysis of the Framer template ecosystem - track new releases, popular categories, pricing trends, and which creators dominate specific niches.

Enter the requested input when prompted. The flow opens the target page, extracts the fields listed above, and saves them as a CSV in your home folder.
