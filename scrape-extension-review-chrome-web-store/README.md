# Chrome Web Store Extension Reviews

User reviews on Chrome Web Store extensions contain some of the most honest product feedback available anywhere. Unlike curated testimonials, these reviews come from real users sharing unfiltered experiences - what they love, what frustrates them, what features they want added, and what bugs they've encountered. Each review includes the reviewer's name, avatar, position on the page, star rating (1-5), full review text, and date posted.

## What it extracts

- **Position** — The ranking position of the review on the page.
- **Name of the reviewer** — Name or username of the reviewer.
- **Avatar URL** — URL link to the reviewer's profile avatar image.
- **Review** — Full review content and text.
- **Date of the review** — When the review was submitted.
- **Star Rating number** — Rating from 1 to 5 stars.

## What you can do with it

- User sentiment analysis: Extract reviews to understand what users genuinely think. Analyze star rating distributions and review text to measure satisfaction levels and identify common complaints.
- Feature request mining: Users often describe desired features in reviews. Extract to catalog feature requests across your extension and competitors' - free product research from your most engaged users.
- Competitive weakness identification: Negative reviews on competitor extensions reveal their pain points. Extract to understand what frustrates users and build those solutions into your own product.
- Quality validation for procurement: Evaluating an extension for your organization? Extract reviews to go beyond the star rating and read what actual users say about reliability, performance, and support.

## How it works

Enter the requested input when prompted. The flow opens the target page, extracts the fields listed above, and saves them as a CSV in your home folder.

## Frequently asked questions

**Can I extract all reviews or just recent ones?**

The robot extracts reviews visible on the page. Chrome Web Store shows recent reviews by default. You can sort by different criteria on the page before extracting.

**Are reviewer names real or anonymous?**

Reviewers use their Google account display names. Some may use full names while others use usernames or initials.

**Can I extract reviews for themes as well?**

This robot is optimized for extension review pages. Theme reviews follow a similar format and may also be extractable.
