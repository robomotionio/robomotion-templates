# Eventbrite Online Events

Eventbrite is the leading platform for event discovery and ticketing, hosting millions of events from small community meetups to large-scale virtual conferences. Its online events category alone spans thousands of webinars, virtual workshops, live streams, and digital conferences across every industry. Each event listing shows the title, event image, and direct link to the event page.

## What it extracts

- **Position** — Ranking position of the event in search results.
- **Title** — Name of the event.
- **Image** — Event listing image or thumbnail.
- **Event link** — Direct URL to the event page on Eventbrite.

## What you can do with it

- Competitive event intelligence: Extract events in your industry. See who's hosting competing events, how they price tickets, and when they schedule programming to differentiate your own events.
- Sponsorship opportunity discovery: Virtual events need sponsors. Extract event listings to identify high-profile events in your target market for sponsorship and partnership outreach.
- Market trend analysis: The types of events being created reflect industry trends. Extract listings over time to track what topics, formats, and pricing models are gaining traction.
- Scheduling optimization: Extract event dates in your niche. Identify scheduling gaps where you can run events without competing against established programming.

## How it works

Enter the requested input when prompted. The flow opens the target page, extracts the fields listed above, and saves them as a CSV in your home folder.

## Frequently asked questions

**Does this extract in-person events too?**

This robot is optimized for online event listings. You can also extract in-person events by navigating to an appropriate Eventbrite search page and pasting that URL.

**Can I extract past events?**

Eventbrite primarily displays upcoming events. Past events may be accessible through organizer pages or specific search filters, depending on organizer settings.

**How often are new events posted?**

Eventbrite has new events posted constantly. Schedule regular extractions to capture new listings in your industry.
