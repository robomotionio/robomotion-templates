# Dribbble Job Board

Dribbble's job board sits within the largest community of professional designers on the internet. Companies that post on Dribbble are not just filling roles - they are signaling to the design community that they value design excellence and want to attract talent from the platform where top designers showcase their work. Listings on Dribbble lean toward product design, UI/UX, brand design, illustration, and motion graphics, with companies ranging from design agencies and startups to enterprises with dedicated design teams.

## What it extracts

- **Position** — The position number in the extraction sequence.
- **Job Title** — The design position title.
- **Company Name** — The hiring organization.
- **Location** — Office location or remote designation.
- **Posted Date (ago)** — When the listing was posted, expressed as time elapsed.
- **Job Link** — Direct URL to the job listing on Dribbble.
- **Company Logo** — Logo image of the hiring organization.

## What you can do with it

- Design-specific job listings from the platform designers trust - companies invest in posting on Dribbble because they want to reach the design community directly.
- Specialty categorization: listings are tagged by design discipline (product, UI/UX, brand, illustration, motion). Filter by your exact specialty.
- Design-focused salary data: when included, salary ranges are specific to design roles - more useful for benchmarking than general tech salary data.
- Remote-friendly design roles: Dribbble's global design community means many listings explicitly support remote work for creative professionals.
