# Dribbble Job Details

Dribbble is not just a portfolio platform - its job board is one of the most curated sources of design and creative job postings on the internet. Companies that hire through Dribbble tend to be design-forward organizations that value craft, and the roles listed skew toward product design, UX/UI, brand design, illustration, and creative direction. For designers tracking the job market, recruiters sourcing design talent, or analysts studying creative industry hiring, Dribbble's job listings contain signal that generic job boards lack.

## What it extracts

- **Job Title** — The position title as listed on Dribbble.
- **Company** — The hiring company.
- **Company Website** — The URL of the hiring company's website.
- **Job Type** — Full-time, part-time, contract, or freelance.
- **Location 1** — Primary office location or remote designation.
- **Location 2** — Secondary office location when applicable.
- **Date Posted** — The date the job listing was published on Dribbble.
- **Description** — The full job description and requirements.
