# JustRemote Jobs

JustRemote focuses on one thing and does it well: verified remote job listings. While general job boards let employers tag any role as remote even when it requires periodic office visits or specific location residency, JustRemote verifies that positions are genuinely remote. The platform covers development, design, marketing, writing, customer support, HR, and project management categories, with listings from companies that have made remote work a real part of their operating model.

## What it extracts

- **Position** — The listing position number or identifier.
- **Company Name** — The hiring organization.
- **Job Title** — The position title.
- **Job Type** — Employment type (full-time, part-time, contract, etc.).
- **Date Posted** — When the job listing was published.
- **Company Logo** — Logo image of the hiring company.
- **Job Link** — Direct URL to the full job listing.

## What you can do with it

- Verified remote positions - JustRemote screens listings so every extracted job is genuinely remote, not hybrid-in-disguise or location-dependent.
- Clean category organization: listings are organized by discipline (development, design, marketing, support, HR) for targeted extraction by specialty.
- Remote type specificity: JustRemote distinguishes between fully remote, partially remote, and remote with travel. Extract these distinctions for accurate job matching.
- Global coverage: JustRemote includes opportunities from companies worldwide, not just US-based employers, expanding your remote job search internationally.

## How it works

Just run the flow. It loads the source, extracts the fields listed above, and saves them as a CSV in your home folder.
