# Trendyol Product Listings

Trendyol's search results and category pages list hundreds of products with pricing and discount indicators - everything a buyer sees when browsing the marketplace. For e-commerce businesses entering or competing in the Turkish market, understanding how products are priced and what discount strategies are common requires looking at the full category picture, not just individual products. This robot extracts multiple product listings from Trendyol search results or category pages at once: product position, names, current prices, discount percentages, product images, and direct links to each listing.

## What it extracts

- **Position** — Product's ranking on the search or category page.
- **Name** — Listing title.
- **Price** — Active selling price in TL.
- **Discount** — Savings percentage indicator.
- **Image URL** — Direct link to product thumbnail image.
- **Link** — URL to the product detail page.

## What you can do with it

- Category price mapping: See the full price range across a product category. Understand where premium, mid-range, and budget products cluster on Trendyol.
- Seller dominance analysis: Count which sellers appear most frequently in category listings. Dominant sellers control visibility and pricing in their categories.
- Discount landscape: Extract original and sale prices across a category to understand how aggressively sellers discount. Some categories run perpetual sales while others rarely discount.
- Market entry intelligence: Before launching products on Trendyol, survey existing listings to understand competitive density, pricing expectations, and customer rating standards.

## How it works

Enter the requested input when prompted. The flow opens the target page, extracts the fields listed above, and saves them as a CSV in your home folder.

**How is this different from the Trendyol detail scraper?**

This robot extracts summary data from multiple products on search or category pages. The detail scraper extracts deep information from one individual product page.

Yes. Apply any Trendyol filter before copying the URL. The robot extracts only products matching your filtered results.

**How many products can it extract per page?**

The robot extracts all products visible on the search results page. Trendyol paginates results - extract each page for full category coverage.

**Does it work for Trendyol Go (grocery)?**

This robot targets Trendyol's main marketplace product listings. Trendyol Go grocery may use a different page structure.
