# Y Combinator Company Details

Y Combinator's startup directory is one of the most valuable resources for tracking innovation - it lists thousands of companies across batches, complete with descriptions, team sizes, industry classifications, and company images. Investors, competitors, recruiters, and researchers all want access to this data, but the directory does not offer a bulk export.

## What it extracts

- **Company Name** — The startup name as listed in the YC directory.
- **Description** — The full company description from the profile.
- **Company Image** — The company logo or thumbnail image.
- **YC Batch** — The YC batch the company participated in (e.g., W24, S23).
- **Ownership Structure** — The company's ownership and equity structure.
- **Tag 1** — The primary industry or category tag assigned to the company.
- **Tag 2** — The secondary industry or category tag assigned to the company.
- **Location** — The company's headquarters location.
- **Company Website** — The official website URL for the company.
- **Founded** — The year the company was founded.
- **Team Size** — The number of employees listed.
- **About the Company** — Additional details and background about the company.
