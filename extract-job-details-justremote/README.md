# JustRemote Job Details

JustRemote is a dedicated remote job board that curates positions across development, design, marketing, customer support, writing, and operations - all verified as genuinely remote. Unlike general job boards that mix in-office roles with a "remote" filter, JustRemote's entire focus is distributed work. For job seekers committed to remote careers, recruiters building distributed teams, or researchers studying the remote work market, JustRemote provides clean signal about what remote roles are available and what they pay.

## What it extracts

- **Job Title** — The position title as listed on JustRemote.
- **Job Type** — Employment type (full-time, part-time, contract, etc.).
- **Location** — Geographic eligibility (worldwide, Americas, Europe, etc.).
- **Company** — The hiring company or organization.
- **Company Link** — Direct URL to the company's website or profile.
- **Company Logo** — Company logo image for visual identification.
- **Company Summary** — Brief description of the company and its mission.
- **Description** — Full job listing details, requirements, and role information.
- **Experience** — Required experience level and qualifications.
- **Salary and Perks** — Compensation range and benefits when disclosed.
- **Link to Apply** — Direct URL to submit your application for the position.
