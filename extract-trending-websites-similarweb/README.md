# Similarweb Trending Websites

Similarweb's trending websites section spotlights domains experiencing the fastest traffic growth over recent weeks. Unlike the static top websites rankings that show established giants, trending lists surface emerging sites, viral phenomena, seasonal surges, and breakout platforms gaining momentum rapidly. Each entry shows the domain position, trending rank, traffic change, and direct link to the site.

## What it extracts

- **Position** — Ranking position on the trending websites list.
- **Trending Up Rank** — The rank number indicating traffic growth tier.
- **Trending Up Domain** — Website URL of the trending site.
- **Trending Up Change** — Percentage or magnitude of traffic change.
- **Trending Up Website Link** — Direct clickable link to the website.
- **Trending Up Favicon** — Website favicon or logo thumbnail.

## What you can do with it

- Early competitor detection: Trending lists reveal fast-growing sites in your category before they reach top rankings. Extract these to stay ahead of emerging competitive threats.
- Viral content discovery: Websites trending due to viral content or media attention appear here first. Extract to identify content strategies that drive rapid traffic growth.
- Investment signal generation: Rapid traffic growth is a leading indicator for business momentum. Extract trending sites to identify potential investment opportunities early.
- Channel opportunity identification: Trending sites often represent new platforms where your audience is migrating. Extract to discover advertising and partnership channels before they get crowded.

## How it works

Just run the flow. It loads the source, extracts the fields listed above, and saves them as a CSV in your home folder.

## Frequently asked questions

**How often does Similarweb update trending data?**

Trending lists typically refresh weekly, reflecting the most recent traffic acceleration signals from Similarweb's web panel data.

**Can I filter trending websites by category?**

Yes. Navigate a specific trending page on Similarweb and paste that URL. The robot extracts the position, rank, domain, change, link, and favicon from whatever the page displays.

**What does the traffic change represent?**

It shows the percentage increase in estimated traffic compared to the previous period, indicating how fast a site's traffic is accelerating.
