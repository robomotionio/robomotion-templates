# Working Nomads Job Details

Working Nomads curates remote job listings with a specific audience in mind: professionals who work while traveling and living in different locations around the world. The platform filters for roles that are truly location-independent - not just work-from-home, but positions compatible with working across time zones, countries, and continents. Categories span development, design, management, marketing, sales, writing, and more, but every listing shares one quality: compatibility with the digital nomad lifestyle.

## What it extracts

- **Job Title** — The position title.
- **Description** — Key information about the position and requirements.
- **Company** — The hiring organization.
- **Company Link** — Direct link to the company's website or profile.
- **Job Type** — Employment type (full-time, part-time, contract, etc.).
- **Location** — Geographic location or remote work region.
- **Applicants** — Number of applicants for the position.
- **Date Posted** — When the listing was published.
- **Link to Apply** — Direct URL to submit an application.
- **Tag 1** — Primary job category (development, design, marketing, etc.).
- **Tag 2** — Secondary job category or skill tag.
- **Tag 3** — Additional job category or skill tag.
- **Tag 4** — Fourth job category or skill tag.
