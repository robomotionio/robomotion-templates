# Airbnb Search Results

Airbnb's search results pages contain a wealth of market data that individual browsing cannot capture efficiently. Each listing card shows the nightly price, overall rating, number of reviews, location, and key details - and a single search for a popular destination can return hundreds of properties. For short-term rental investors evaluating markets, property managers benchmarking their pricing, hospitality researchers studying platform dynamics, and travel planners comparing options at scale, extracting Airbnb search results transforms manual browsing into structured market intelligence.

## What it extracts

- **Position** — The ranking position of the listing in the search results.
- **Title** — The property name as displayed in results.
- **Name of Place** — The specific location or neighborhood of the listing.
- **Price** — The displayed price per night for the searched dates.
- **Rating** — The overall star rating from guests.
- **Review** — The total number of guest reviews.
- **Main Image** — The primary photo displayed for the listing.
- **Full Information** — Complete listing details and description.
- **Link** — The direct URL to the individual listing page.

## What you can do with it

- Multiple property listings from one extraction - capture entire search result pages instead of clicking through listings one at a time.
- Price comparison at scale: extract nightly rates across dozens or hundreds of properties to understand market pricing for a specific destination and date range.
- Rating and review intelligence: guest ratings and review counts reveal listing quality and popularity. Extract this data to identify top-performing properties in any market.
- Property type analysis: Airbnb lists entire homes, private rooms, shared rooms, and unique stays. Extract property types to understand the supply mix in any market.

## How it works

Enter the requested input when prompted. The flow opens the target page, extracts the fields listed above, and saves them as a CSV in your home folder.
