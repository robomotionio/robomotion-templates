# Workable Job Search

Workable is one of the most popular applicant tracking systems (ATS) used by thousands of companies worldwide. Many organizations use Workable to host their public job boards, meaning Workable's search results aggregate open positions across these companies. Each listing in Workable search results shows the job title, job link, company name, location (including remote options), job type, employment type, and posting recency.

## What it extracts

- **Position** — Job listing position number or identifier.
- **Job Name** — Position title and seniority level.
- **Job Link** — Direct URL to the full job posting.
- **Type** — Job category or classification.
- **Location** — Office location or remote indicator.
- **Employment Type** — Full-time, part-time, or contract.
- **Company Name** — Hiring company name.
- **Date Posted** — When the position was listed.

## What you can do with it

- Startup hiring intelligence: Workable is especially popular with startups and scale-ups. Extract job listings to see which companies are expanding and what roles they need.
- Multi-company job monitoring: Instead of checking individual company career pages, extract Workable search results to see aggregated openings across many employers.
- Department growth signals: Job listings by department reveal where companies are investing. Multiple engineering posts signals product development; sales posts indicate growth focus.
- Remote work tracking: Workable listings indicate remote, hybrid, or on-site requirements. Extract location data to track remote work adoption trends across companies.

**Does Workable aggregate jobs from many companies?**

Yes. Companies use Workable as their ATS, and Workable's search aggregates public positions across all these employers.

**Can I filter by remote jobs only?**

Yes. Use Workable's search filters for remote positions before copying the URL. The robot extracts from the filtered results.
