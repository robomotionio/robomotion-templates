# Meetup Events Near Location

Meetup hosts millions of local events across thousands of cities, covering professional networking, technology, entrepreneurship, fitness, outdoor activities, language exchange, creative arts, and hundreds of other interests. When you search Meetup for events near a location, the results display event positions, event names, the organizing group, the scheduled date and time, current attendee count (RSVPs), remaining spots available, event images, and direct links to each event. These fields together paint a vivid picture of the community activity landscape in any geography.

## What it extracts

- **Position** — Ranking or position number of the event in the search results.
- **Name** — Title of the Meetup event.
- **Date** — Scheduled date and start time of the event.
- **Group** — Name of the group hosting the event.
- **Attendees** — Number of RSVPs at time of extraction.
- **Spots Left** — Number of remaining spots available for the event.
- **Image** — Event listing image or thumbnail.
- **Link** — Direct URL to the event page on Meetup.

## What you can do with it

- Local community discovery: Extract events near your city to surface active professional and interest communities. Identify the groups most relevant to your industry or goals - and the organizers building them - without manually clicking through dozens of Meetup pages.
- Event scheduling intelligence: Attendee counts reveal which event formats and topics are actually drawing people. Extract to understand what's pulling crowds in your space before planning your own events and choosing dates that don't collide with established programming.
- Networking opportunity identification: Extract events in your professional category near a target location. Build a calendar of networking events relevant to your industry for attending, sponsoring, or partnering with as a speaker or venue.
- Community health analysis: Organizers and researchers can extract Meetup event data over time to measure community vitality - are more events being created, are RSVPs growing, are new groups emerging in a city or category?

## How it works

Enter the requested input when prompted. The flow opens the target page, extracts the fields listed above, and saves them as a CSV in your home folder.

## Frequently asked questions

**Can I search Meetup events by category as well as location?**

Yes. Meetup's search lets you filter by category - Technology, Business, Arts, Sports, and more. Apply a category filter before copying the URL so the robot extracts only events in your area of interest.

**Does this capture online events as well as in-person?**

Meetup lists both in-person and online events. Online events typically show 'Online Event' as the venue. If you want only local in-person events, filter the search on Meetup before copying the URL.

**How far in advance are Meetup events posted?**

Groups post events anywhere from a few days to several months ahead. For a forward-looking event calendar, run the extraction and sort by date to see upcoming scheduling across any time horizon the search covers.
