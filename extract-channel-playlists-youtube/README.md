# YouTube Channel Playlists

YouTube playlists reveal how creators organize their content - which topics they group together, how deep they go on specific subjects, and what series or collections they are building. For competitive analysis, content strategy research, and influencer evaluation, playlist data is a goldmine that most tools overlook.

## What it extracts

- **Position** — The numerical rank of the playlist on the channel's playlists tab.
- **Title** — The name of the playlist as displayed on the channel.
- **Number of Videos** — The total number of videos currently in the playlist.
- **Image** — The thumbnail image URL representing the playlist.
- **Link** — The direct URL to the playlist page on YouTube.

## What you can do with it

- A complete view of any channel's content organization - every playlist with its title, video count, and update status - in one structured export.
- Competitor content strategy analysis: see exactly which topic buckets competitors invest in and how many videos they dedicate to each.
- Content gap identification by comparing your playlists against competitor playlists to find topics they cover that you do not.
- Sponsorship research that goes beyond subscriber counts - understand what content series a creator maintains and which are actively growing.
