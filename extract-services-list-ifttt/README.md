# IFTTT Services

IFTTT (If This Then That) connects over 800 services through a simple trigger-and-action automation model, making it one of the broadest integration platforms available. The IFTTT services directory lists every connected service with its position in the directory, service name, direct link to the service page, and service logo image.

## What it extracts

- **Position** — Numerical ranking of the service in the directory listing.
- **Service Name** — Connected platform or device name.
- **Service URL** — Link to the service's IFTTT page.
- **Service Image** — Logo or thumbnail image representing the service.

## What you can do with it

- Integration landscape mapping: See every service IFTTT connects with in one dataset. Understand the full scope of automation possibilities across 800+ platforms.
- Trigger and action discovery: Each service has specific triggers and actions. Extract these to understand exactly what can be automated with each connected service.
- Popular integration identification: Applet counts indicate which services have the most automation usage. High applet counts signal services with strong integration demand.
- Category analysis: Services are grouped by category. Extract categories to see which areas (smart home, productivity, social media, etc.) have the most integration options.

## How it works

Just run the flow. It loads the source, extracts the fields listed above, and saves them as a CSV in your home folder.
