# CoinMarketCap Category Coins

CoinMarketCap organizes thousands of cryptocurrencies into categories - DeFi, Layer 1, Gaming, NFTs, Meme Coins, and hundreds more. These category pages are valuable for understanding which sectors of the crypto market are growing, which are losing steam, and where new projects are launching. But there is no export button on CoinMarketCap's category pages, and copying data for dozens of coins manually is not realistic for serious analysis.

## What it extracts

- **Crypto Category** — The sector or category classification (e.g., DeFi, Layer 1, Gaming).
- **Currency Name** — The full name of the cryptocurrency.
- **Symbol** — The trading ticker symbol (e.g., BTC, ETH, SOL).
- **Price** — The current price at the time of extraction.
- **Market Cap** — The total market capitalization of the coin.
- **Trading Volume** — The total trading volume metric for the coin.
- **24h Volume Dollar** — The total trading volume in USD over the last 24 hours.
- **24h Volume Coin** — The total trading volume in coin units over the last 24 hours.
- **Position** — The position or rank within the category listing.
- **Ranking** — The overall ranking of the cryptocurrency.
- **1 Hour Status** — The price movement direction over the last 1 hour.
- **1 Hour Value** — The percentage price change over the last 1 hour.
- **24 Hours Status** — The price movement direction over the last 24 hours.
- **24 Hours Value** — The percentage price change over the last 24 hours.
- **7 Days Status** — The price movement direction over the last 7 days.
- **7 Days Value** — The percentage price change over the last 7 days.
- **Coin Market Cap** — The market cap value for the individual coin.
- **Circulating Supply** — The number of coins currently in circulation.
