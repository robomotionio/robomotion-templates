# AppSumo Product Questions

AppSumo is the go-to marketplace for lifetime software deals, offering discounts on SaaS tools, digital products, and business software that can reach 90% off regular pricing. The Q&A sections on AppSumo product pages are goldmines of unfiltered customer intelligence. Potential buyers ask pointed questions about features, limitations, integrations, pricing after the deal expires, refund policies, and use case compatibility.

## What it extracts

- **Position** — Ranking order of the question in the Q&A section.
- **Question** — Full text of the customer question.
- **Details** — Additional context, clarifications, or question metadata.
- **User** — Username of the person who asked the question.
- **Date** — Timestamp when the question was posted.
- **Link** — Direct URL to the specific question or Q&A thread.

## What you can do with it

- Unfiltered buyer concerns: Questions reveal what potential customers worry about before purchasing. These are the real objections that marketing pages don't address.
- Feature confirmation: Buyers ask specific questions about capabilities. Founder responses confirm or deny features, giving you verified information beyond marketing claims.
- Competitive intelligence: Study what questions buyers ask about competitor products on AppSumo. Understand the feature gaps and concerns driving purchase decisions.
- Product roadmap hints: Founders frequently mention upcoming features and timelines in Q&A responses. Extract these to track product development direction.
