# AppSumo Deals

AppSumo is the dominant marketplace for lifetime software deals, offering SaaS tools and digital products at deeply discounted one-time prices instead of recurring subscriptions. Its product listing pages display deal names, discounted prices, original prices, category classifications, detailed descriptions, total review counts, product images, and direct links to each listing. For software buyers, AppSumo is a goldmine - but discovering the best deals requires sorting through hundreds of active listings across dozens of categories.

## What it extracts

- **Products Count** — Total number of products extracted from the page.
- **Position** — Ranking position of the product on the listing page.
- **Product Name** — Name of the deal or software tool.
- **Category** — Product category or tag classification.
- **Category Link** — Direct URL link to the product category.
- **Discounted Price** — Current AppSumo lifetime deal price.
- **Original Price** — Full or annual subscription price before discount.
- **Product Link** — Direct URL link to the individual product listing.
- **Description** — Summary description of the software tool or deal.
- **Reviews** — Total number of buyer reviews.
- **Image** — Product thumbnail or logo image.

## What you can do with it

- Deal discovery at scale: Extract all active AppSumo listings at once. Build a complete catalog of available deals with pricing, discounts, and ratings so you can compare options without clicking through each page individually.
- Competitive pricing intelligence: SaaS founders use AppSumo to gauge what customers will pay for tools in their category. Extract listings to benchmark your product's pricing against comparable deals in the marketplace.
- Investment and market signal tracking: High review counts on deals signal strong buyer interest. Extract regularly to track which categories are gaining traction and which deals are generating the most community engagement.
- Deal alert database: Schedule regular extractions to monitor new deals as they launch. Build a database of deal launches over time to analyze AppSumo's deal cadence and category focus.

## How it works

Just run the flow. It loads the source, extracts the fields listed above, and saves them as a CSV in your home folder.

## Frequently asked questions

**Does this extract expired or past AppSumo deals?**

This robot extracts active deals from AppSumo's current listings pages. Past and expired deals are generally no longer listed on the main browse pages, though they may appear on specific archive views.

**Can I filter by category or price before extracting?**

Yes. Apply filters on AppSumo's browse page - by category, price range, or sort order - and copy that filtered URL. The robot extracts whatever the page displays.

**How often do new AppSumo deals launch?**

AppSumo typically launches new deals multiple times per week. Schedule regular extractions to capture new arrivals and monitor the deal calendar over time.
