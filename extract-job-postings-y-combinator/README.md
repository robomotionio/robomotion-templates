# Y Combinator Jobs

Y Combinator's Work at a Startup board is the definitive hiring platform for the world's most ambitious startups. Every company listed has been vetted and backed by YC - the accelerator behind Stripe, Airbnb, DoorDash, Coinbase, and thousands of other companies. The job board aggregates open positions across all active YC companies, from brand-new batch companies with five employees to late-stage unicorns with thousands.

## What it extracts

- **Job Category** — The category or type of role (e.g., Engineering, Sales, Product).
- **Position** — The sequential position number in the listing.
- **Company Name** — The YC-backed startup hiring for the role.
- **Company Description** — Brief description of what the startup does.
- **Job Title** — The position title or job name.
- **Employment Type** — Full-time, part-time, contract, or other arrangement.
- **Location** — Office location or remote policy.
- **Specialization** — Specific skill area or department focus for the role.
- **Company Logo** — Logo image of the YC-backed startup.
- **Application URL** — Direct link to apply for the position.

## What you can do with it

- Startup jobs vetted by the world's top accelerator - every company on the board has gone through YC's rigorous selection process, providing a quality filter unavailable on general job boards.
- Funding stage visibility: see whether a startup is pre-seed, Series A, or growth stage. Match your risk tolerance to the right company maturity level.
- Team size intelligence: YC lists current team sizes, revealing which startups are early (you would be employee #10) versus scaling (joining a 200-person team).
- Tech stack data: engineering roles on the YC board frequently list the tech stack, giving you a preview of the tools and languages you would work with.

## Frequently asked questions

**What is Y Combinator's Work at a Startup?**

It is Y Combinator's official job board that aggregates open positions from all active YC-backed companies. Only companies that have been through the YC program can list jobs.

**Are all companies on the YC board startups?**

They all started as YC companies, but some have grown to hundreds or thousands of employees. The board includes everything from 5-person seed-stage companies to large-scale businesses.

**Can I filter by role type or location?**

Yes. The YC board offers filters for role type, location, industry, and more. Apply filters before copying the URL.

**How often are new jobs posted?**

YC companies post continuously, with spikes around YC Demo Days when new batches of companies begin hiring their first employees.
