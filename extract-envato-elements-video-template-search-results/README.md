# Envato Elements Video Templates

Motion graphics templates save video editors hours of work - but finding the right one on Envato Elements can take nearly as long as building from scratch. The platform hosts thousands of After Effects projects, Premiere Pro templates, DaVinci Resolve presets, and motion graphic packs. Searching for something specific returns pages of results, and comparing options means opening tabs, reading descriptions one at a time, and tracking which templates caught your eye.

## What it extracts

- **Position** — The ranking number of the template in the search results.
- **Video Template Thumbnail** — The preview image representing the template.
- **Video Template Link** — Direct link to the template detail page on Envato Elements.
- **Video Template Title** — The title of the video template as listed on Envato Elements.
- **Creator** — The designer or studio that produced the template.
- **Creator Link** — Direct link to the creator's profile on Envato Elements.

## What you can do with it

- Complete search result capture from Envato Elements - every video template listing with name, software compatibility, category, and creator in one structured export.
- Software-specific filtering so your After Effects editors never waste time on Premiere-only templates and vice versa. Extract the data, filter the column, shortlist in seconds.
- Collaborative template selection that replaces link-sharing with a shared spreadsheet. Your team reviews options, adds notes, and picks winners without visiting Envato Elements individually.
- Trend monitoring for motion graphics by scheduling periodic extractions. Track which template categories, styles, and software formats are gaining popularity on the platform.

## How it works

Enter the requested input when prompted. The flow opens the target page, extracts the fields listed above, and saves them as a CSV in your home folder.

## Frequently asked questions

**Can I filter results by editing software before extracting?**

Yes. Apply the software filter on the Envato Elements search page before copying the URL. The robot extracts whatever the filtered results display, so you only get templates compatible with your software.

**Does this robot download the actual template files?**

No. It extracts metadata - template titles, thumbnails, creator information, and direct links - from the search results page. You download the actual files through your Envato Elements subscription.

**Can I track new template uploads over time?**

Yes. Schedule the robot to run on the same search URL at a regular interval. Compare extractions to identify newly added templates that were not present in earlier runs.
