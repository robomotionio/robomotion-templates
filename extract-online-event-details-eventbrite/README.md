# Eventbrite Event Details

Eventbrite hosts millions of events annually, and its online events section has grown dramatically as virtual conferences, webinars, workshops, and networking sessions became standard. Each Eventbrite event page packs detailed information - event title, date and time across time zones, location or virtual meeting details, ticket pricing, and full event description. For event industry professionals tracking competitive programming, marketers identifying sponsorship or speaking opportunities, and researchers studying the virtual events ecosystem, manually reviewing these detail pages is impractical when you need to evaluate dozens or hundreds of events.

## What it extracts

- **Name** — Full event name.
- **Date and Time** — Event date, start time, end time, and time zone.
- **Location** — Event location or virtual meeting details.
- **About Event** — Full event description and agenda.
- **Price** — Ticket pricing information.
- **Refund** — Refund policy details.

## What you can do with it

- Competitive event analysis: Extract details from events in your industry niche to understand how competitors structure their programming, price their tickets, and position their offerings.
- Sponsorship and speaking prospecting: Identify upcoming events in your space that could be partnership opportunities. Extract organizer information and event themes systematically.
- Pricing intelligence: Compare ticket pricing across similar events. Understand the market rate for virtual conferences, workshops, and webinars in your industry.
- Event trend monitoring: Track which types of online events are being created - topics, formats, pricing models - to understand where the virtual events market is heading.

## How it works

Enter the requested input when prompted. The flow opens the target page, extracts the fields listed above, and saves them as a CSV in your home folder.
