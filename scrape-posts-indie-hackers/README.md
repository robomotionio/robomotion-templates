# Indie Hackers Posts

Indie Hackers is where bootstrapped founders come to share revenue milestones, ask hard questions, and document the messy reality of building a business without venture capital. Unlike polished press releases or LinkedIn updates, posts on Indie Hackers are refreshingly candid - founders announcing their first $1K month, asking for advice on churn, sharing failed experiments, and celebrating new product launches. Each post surfaces a title, author, publication date, upvote count, comment count, and topic tags.

## What it extracts

- **Post Title** — Headline of the post.
- **Author** — Username of the post creator.
- **Upvote Count** — Total community upvotes received.
- **Comment Count** — Number of replies and comments.
- **Date Posted** — When the post was published.
- **Topic Tag** — Category or topic classification.
- **Post URL** — Direct link to the full post.

## What you can do with it

- Community trend tracking: Extract top posts over time to see which topics - revenue milestones, growth tactics, product launches, mental health - are gaining community traction. Align your own content and messaging with what the community cares about most.
- Content marketing intelligence: The posts that earn hundreds of upvotes on Indie Hackers reveal what resonates with an audience of founders and makers. Extract to build a swipe file of winning angles, titles, and story structures.
- Competitive founder research: Track posts from founders in your product category. See what they're building, what challenges they're sharing, and which milestones they're celebrating to benchmark your own growth.
- Product validation signal: Posts asking 'has anyone built X?' or 'what tools do you use for Y?' represent unmet needs. Extract community discussions to identify product opportunities validated by real founder demand.

## How it works

Just run the flow. It loads the source, extracts the fields listed above, and saves them as a CSV in your home folder.

## Frequently asked questions

**Can I extract posts from specific topic tags?**

Yes. Navigate to a topic-filtered view on Indie Hackers - such as 'Marketing', 'Product', or 'Revenue' - and paste that URL. The robot extracts posts from whatever page you point it at.

**Can I track a single founder's posts over time?**

The robot extracts listing pages, not individual profiles. For profile-level tracking, navigate to a founder's public profile page on Indie Hackers and use that URL.

**How often should I re-run this robot?**

Indie Hackers receives new posts daily. For trend tracking, weekly extractions provide a reliable longitudinal dataset without excessive API usage.
