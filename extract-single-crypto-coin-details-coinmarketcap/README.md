# CoinMarketCap Coin Details

CoinMarketCap is the most-referenced cryptocurrency data aggregator, tracking over 2 million tokens and coins across hundreds of exchanges. Each coin detail page on CoinMarketCap consolidates key market metrics in one view: current price, market capitalization, fully diluted valuation, 24-hour trading volume, circulating supply versus max supply, price change status and percentages across multiple timeframes (1h, 24h, 7d, 30d, 90d, 1y), market cap growth metrics, volume-to-market-cap ratios, circulating supply percentages, and direct links to the currency's website, GitHub repository, and whitepaper. CoinMarketCap also displays exchange listings, historical data, and community metrics.

## What it extracts

- **Currency** — Name of the cryptocurrency.
- **Symbol** — Ticker symbol of the coin.
- **Price** — Current market price in USD.
- **24h Change Status** — Direction of price movement over 24 hours (up/down).
- **24h Change Rate** — Percentage price change over the last 24 hours.
- **Market Cap** — Price multiplied by circulating supply.
- **Market Cap Growth Status** — Direction of market cap movement.
- **Market Cap Growth Rate** — Percentage change in market cap.
- **Volume 24h** — Total trading volume in the last 24 hours.
- **Volume Over Market Cap 24h** — Ratio of 24h volume to market cap.
- **Circulating Supply** — Coins currently in the market.
- **Circulating Supply Percentage** — Percentage of total supply in circulation.
- **Total Supply** — All coins that exist (including locked/reserved).
- **Max Supply** — Hard cap on total coins that can ever exist.
- **Fully Diluted Market Cap** — Market cap calculated at max supply.
- **Currency Website** — Official website URL for the cryptocurrency.
- **Currency GitHub** — GitHub repository link for the project.
- **Currency Whitepaper** — Link to the project's whitepaper document.

## What you can do with it

- Real-time market metrics: Extract current price, market cap, and 24h volume from any coin page. Get the exact data CoinMarketCap displays without manual recording.
- Supply economics at a glance: Circulating supply, total supply, and max supply tell the token's inflation story. Extract these to evaluate scarcity and future dilution risk.
- Multi-timeframe price context: CoinMarketCap shows price changes across six timeframes. Extract all of them to understand whether a coin is in short-term or long-term momentum.
- All-time high/low benchmarking: See how far a coin is from its peak and floor. Extract ATH/ATL data to gauge where a coin sits in its historical price range.
