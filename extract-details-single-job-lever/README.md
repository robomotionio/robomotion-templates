# Lever Job Details

Lever is one of the most popular applicant tracking systems, powering the career pages of thousands of technology companies - from startups to enterprises. Job postings on Lever contain rich structured data: titles, descriptions, requirement lists, team information, location details, employment types, and application deadlines. For recruiters tracking competitor hiring, job seekers comparing opportunities, or analysts studying labor market trends, this data is valuable but scattered across individual postings.

## What it extracts

- **Job Title** — The title of the position.
- **Job Location** — The work location (office, remote, or hybrid).
- **Team** — The team or department the role belongs to.
- **Employment Type** — Full-time, part-time, contract, or internship.
- **Job Type** — The classification or category of the job.
- **About** — The full job description and company context text.
- **Role Responsibilities** — The listed duties and expectations for the position.
- **Skills you bring** — The required skills, experience, and education.
- **Additional Ability** — Extra qualifications, certifications, or nice-to-have skills.
- **Deadline** — The application deadline or posting expiration date.

- Complete job postings extracted from Lever - titles, descriptions, requirements, and locations in structured format for easy comparison.
