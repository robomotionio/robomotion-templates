# Built In Top Companies

Built In is a leading technology and startup-focused career platform that publishes influential annual "Best Places to Work" rankings across multiple US tech hubs - including Austin, Boston, Chicago, Colorado, Dallas, Los Angeles, New York, San Francisco, and Seattle. These rankings, compiled from employee survey data, list companies by size category (small, midsize, large, enterprise) with details including the company name, industry vertical, employee count, headquarters location, company profile page, and job listings. Being ranked on Built In's "Best Places to Work" list is a significant employer branding signal in the tech industry.

## What it extracts

- **Position** — Ranking number in the list.
- **Company** — Employer name.
- **Company Page** — Direct link to the company's profile page.
- **Industries** — Business sectors or verticals.
- **Location** — Headquarters city and state.
- **Employees** — Company size or employee count range.
- **Description** — Company overview and key information.
- **All Jobs** — Link to the company's job listings.

## What you can do with it

- Employer ranking intelligence: Extract ranked companies to see which tech employers are rated highest by their own employees. Structured data makes comparison easy.
- Benefits benchmarking: Built In highlights perks and benefits that earned companies their ranking. Extract these to see what top employers offer - and where your company should invest.
- Tech ecosystem mapping: Extract company lists by city to understand each tech hub's employer landscape. See which industries and company sizes dominate each market.
- Job targeting: Job seekers can extract entire "Best Places to Work" lists and filter by industry, size, and location to build targeted employer shortlists.
