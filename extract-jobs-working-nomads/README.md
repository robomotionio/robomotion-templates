# Working Nomads Jobs

Working Nomads is built for a specific audience: people who work while living and traveling around the world. The platform's curation process filters for roles that are genuinely compatible with the digital nomad lifestyle - not just work-from-home positions that require you to stay in one city, but roles that allow you to work from Bali, Lisbon, Mexico City, or wherever your travels take you. This robot extracts multiple listings from Working Nomads' board at once, capturing job titles, companies, categories, locations, job types, direct job links, company links, and skill tags.

## What it extracts

- **Position** — The listing position number.
- **Job Title** — The position title.
- **Company Name** — The hiring organization.
- **Category** — Job category (development, design, marketing, management, etc.).
- **Posted Date (ago)** — How long ago the listing was published.
- **Location** — Geographic region or remote availability.
- **Job Type** — Employment type (full-time, part-time, contract, etc.).
- **Job Link** — Direct URL to the job posting.
- **Company Link** — Direct URL to the company profile.
- **Tag 1** — First skill or requirement tag.
- **Tag 2** — Second skill or requirement tag.
- **Tag 3** — Third skill or requirement tag.
