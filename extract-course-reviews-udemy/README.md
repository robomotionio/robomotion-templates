# Udemy Course Reviews

Student reviews are the most honest signal of course quality on Udemy. They reveal what learners found valuable, what confused them, and whether the course delivered on its promises. But Udemy does not let you export reviews, and scrolling through hundreds of comments manually to find patterns is impractical.

## What it extracts

- **Course Name** — The title of the Udemy course where the review was posted.
- **Position** — The sequential position of the review on the course page.
- **Rating** — The star rating given by the reviewer (1–5 stars).
- **Reviewer** — The display name of the student who left the review.
- **Reviews** — The full text of the student's review.
- **Posted at** — When the review was posted.

## What you can do with it

- Every student review from a course extracted with text, star rating, reviewer name, and date - ready for filtering and analysis.
- Learner satisfaction analysis: identify the most common praise and complaints across hundreds of reviews instead of reading them one by one.
- Course quality comparison: extract reviews from multiple competing courses and compare sentiment themes to find the best option.
- Curriculum improvement insights: mine negative reviews from your own course to find specific lectures or topics that need work.

## How it works

Enter the requested input when prompted. The flow opens the target page, extracts the fields listed above, and saves them as a CSV in your home folder.
